Facts & Use

Suggested Use:

Adults, as a dietary supplement, take one serving (one scoop) mixed with 10-14 ounces of water 20-30 minutes prior to training. Drink immediately after mixing for best flavor. Do not exceed one serving per 24 hour period. Do not combine with foods or supplements containing more than 50mg of caffeine.

WARNING: Consult a medical doctor before taking this supplement if you have or suspect a medical condition or are taking any medications. Contains approximately the same amount of caffeine as two cups of coffee (200mg). Do not use near bedtime, if you're pregnant, nursing, or are sensitive to caffeine. Discontinue taking this product if you experience an adverse event. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. Store in a cool, dry place.
